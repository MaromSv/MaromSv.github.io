Happy Plant

Happy Plant is a smart IOT device and webapp that help users know when and how much water they need to give to their house plants. The physical device consists a moisture sensor and embedded harware to communicate with the webapp. In the webapp, there is a database containing the most common house plants. The user can speficy the plant that they are using the device for and get personalized alerts depening on the plant and its specific needs.
